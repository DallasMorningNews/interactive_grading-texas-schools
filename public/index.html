Grading Texas Schools

See if your school gets an A or an F on Texas’ new report card

By Ariana Giorgi | Staff

Published January 9, 2017

    Texas is moving to a new system in 2018 that assigns public schools and districts grades from A-F based on how well they’re educating kids, as determined mainly by test scores.
